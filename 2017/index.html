Portland Civic Design Festival 2017 - #HackForChange

September 23rd


Come create.

Help folks.

Build community.

Join us for a day long community project prototyping marathon hosted by The Maine Charitable Mechanics Association, Code For Maine, and Portland Global Shapers. September 23rd marks the National Day of Civic Hacking, when people around the country gather to "hack" their cities in a positive way by working on creative and innovative solutions to community challenges. Expect a day of free-spirited collaboration with teams of locals to design and launch new projects for our community. All ages, backgrounds, skill levels welcome! Both high-tech & low-tech projects encouraged!

Participate

How can you help?

"Civic hacking" requires all kinds of skills: high tech, low tech, artistic, or otherwise! No matter what your skills, no matter how much experience you have, we want you!

Join us on May 31!

The most obvious way to participate is to register for the event over the weekend of May 31 - June 1.

Help organize

There are many important things that must get done for the event to be a success. Please join us on Github where we manage tasks.

Sponsor the event

Please consider sponsoring us via either food, funds or space to make this year's event a huge success.

Sponsorship

Help us help Maine.

We are all volunteers. Even volunteers need coffee and food when they spend a weekend hacking on projects that will improve civic life across the pine tree state. Consider sponsoring our event.
